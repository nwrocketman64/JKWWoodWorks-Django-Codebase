About JKW Woodworks

JKW Woodworks goal is to provide quality handmade projects that can be handed down from generation to generation. JKW Woodworks started out as a simply hobby. We were tired of goods and products from foreign countries that would break soon after purchase. We wanted sturdy, hand crafted goods that would stand the test of time. That is our number one priority when designing and creating wood projects.

One of signature projects is the American Flag. We make standard American Flags, Thin Blue Line Flags, Thin Red Line Flags, Thin Green Line Flags, and many more for every occupation. Each flag has a meaning and sends a message of support to our first responders and military. We have two sizes that work for any occasion. The small American Flag is perfect for offices and work spaces. The large flag is a master piece that can be place on the wall like any other painting. These flags can make a simple yet profound statement that shows your support and love of the country.

Another signature project of ours is the epoxy tables. These tables range in all sizes from small end tables to large coffee and dining tables. Because of the epoxy, different colors and designs are endless and each table is unique. These tables take time to make due to the curing process of the epoxy but will last a life time. After we saw different epoxy projects on social media, we decided to give it a shot. And we nailed it! Epoxy can be tricky and expensive but when done right, it creates a strong, naturally beautiful piece of art that is very durable and will make a statement.

We work with all sorts of different lumber ranging from pine or dimensional lumber to cedar, cherry, and walnut. Each piece is unique and made to the customers desires.

We have many different plans and designs for more projects. We are constantly improving our designs and the way we construct items so the best possible piece is created every time. Feel free to contact us about any ideas or questions you may have. We are always looking for feedback and new ideas that will help us improve. We enjoy making great wood projects and hope you find joy in our work as well.

- JKW Woodworks Team
